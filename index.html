About

Quality Music Lessons at an Affordable Price

A Fun and Energetic Environment to Learn and Play Music

We offer 30 minute one-on-one lessons for students ages 5 to 99 years old. $25.00 per lesson or at a discount rate of $20 per lesson if the entire month is paid by the 1st week (restrictions apply). We accept cash or check only.

We have over 300 students enrolled at Millan Music. We provide a fun and energetic atmosphere for students to learn and develop their musical talent.

We have eight rooms used for music instruction and vocal training. To pass the time we have three waiting rooms (each with a TV), a playroom (complete with toys) and free coffee, cappuccino, hot chocolate and tea.
